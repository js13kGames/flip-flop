Installing Chips

  1. Pick an empty spot on the board.
  2. Pick a chip to install.
  3. Pick an installed chip.
  4. Push the power button.

Reminders

At least every fourth chip needs power.

Glitchy chips may change when installed.

Higher number chip ⇒ less glitchy.
